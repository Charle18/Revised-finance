Financial Tips

Pay Yourself First

Set aside at least 20% of your income for savings and investments before spending on anything else. This ensures you're building wealth with every paycheck.

Emergency Fund Essentials

Aim to save 3-6 months' worth of living expenses in an easily accessible account. This financial cushion can help you weather unexpected expenses without going into debt.

Debt Snowball Method

Pay off debts from smallest to largest balance while making minimum payments on all others. The psychological wins will keep you motivated to become debt-free.
